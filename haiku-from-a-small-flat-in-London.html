Haiku from a small flat in London

Leaves stir in their sleep

Night floods through open windows

And we lie in it

A shape carved in air

Breaking up as it is made

A loud noise stopping

Polystyrene tiles

She stands and when she calls out

No one notices

You may be missing

Out have you registered are

You sure please accept

Stood too close to hear

Anything but your breathing

Sweat drips from ceilings

The sky is concrete

The sun is a buzzing tube

The river slides shut

A man eats sugar

From the bag with licked fingers

Shining mouth smiling
